What is HTML?

Hypertext Markup Language (HTML) is the standard markup language for creating web pages and web applications.

Versions of HTML

  • HTML was published in the year 1991

  • HTML 2.0 was published in the year 1995

  • HTML 3.2 was published in the year 1997

  • HTML 4.01 was published in the year 1999

  • XHTML was published in the year 2000

  • HTML 5 was published in the year 2014
